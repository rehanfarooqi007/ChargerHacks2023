Why should you recycle?

Recycling trash when possible has significant benefits for our environment. Recycling reduces the amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators, conserves energy, and prevents pollution. The world produces about 2.01 billion tons of solid waste a year, and approximately 75% of the world's garbage is estimated to be recyclable, but less than 20% of waste is recycled per year.
